Evaluation of web graphics

Site evaluated: Apple Website

Purpose of graphics on this site:

The graphics on the Apple website are very clean and well done. They effectively show the Apple products in an interesting way that makes you want to buy them. The graphics add to the website by having a contrast between the photos of their products and the navigation tab and background color of the site. The site only has three large photos that stand out and they do a great job at drawing your attention to the focal point of the photo. The photos also do a good job of explaining visually. For example, the Apple Watch photo includes the watches in water showing that they are water proof.

Guidelines for effective use of graphics

  1. Yes- The graphics of the site fit very well with the purpose, organization, and style of the page.
  2. No- Side-to-side images are used at a high resolution.
  3. Yes- The graphics that are used help guide users to the important content of a webpage.
  4. Yes- The images that are used avoid being "obnoxious" but remain being "eye catching".
  5. Yes- While their is text on the graphics, it is not very important and is more of a style choice.
  6. Yes- When you click on the images it gives an in depth explanation of the product
  7. Yes- There is enough contrast between the text and the background of the images.
